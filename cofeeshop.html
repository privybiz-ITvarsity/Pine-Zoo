WILD THINGS COFFEE SHOP

The coffee culture at the Coffee Shop at the zoo is booming,caffeine addicts are starting to know their Robusta from their Arabica.The Coffee Shop knows how to do coffee and how to do coffee well.

The Coffee Shop's baristas latte art is snap.It has interesting décor, decadent displays of food and artistic atmospheres that act as the perfect Instagram background for foodies and selfies.They have a have a rich and strong background in the dark brown liquid gold.

It opened its doors and said “hello” to the people of the zoo in May 2018 and alongside the modern design and interior of the 105 building and its occupiers, this coffee shop focuses on contemporary food in a beautifully curated space. Apart from a seriously good cup of coffee, they also do ‘farmacy’ smoothies, have a vegan-and-vegetarian-friendly menu (while still attending to the meat eaters) and the bar seating with plug sockets make for the perfect working space. They often host wine-and-food pairing events as well as a coffee-and-cake pairing that allows you to dive into the balance, body and complexity of coffees.
